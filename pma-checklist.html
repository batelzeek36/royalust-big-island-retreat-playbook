ROYALUST × PMA Checklist — Hawaiʻi Island (Ag Land)

Private Membership Architecture

1) STRUCTURE (KEEP YOUR EXISTING ENTITIES)
  • Keep JASMINE HILL CAPITAL (LLLP) as land/asset owner.
  • Keep ROYALUST LLC for brand + any public-facing/online offerings.
  • Create a SEPARATE PMA (unincorporated private membership association) for member-only retreats.
  • IRS EIN application (free): https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/get-an-employer-identification-number

2) PMA PAPERWORK
  • Draft: founding charter/bylaws, membership agreement, assumption-of-risk & liability waiver, code of conduct, privacy/confidentiality.
  • Get an EIN for the PMA (IRS link above) and open a dedicated bank account.

3) INTER-ENTITY AGREEMENTS
  • Lease, license, or use agreement: LLLP/LLC → PMA (scope, dates, indemnities, maintenance, insurance).
  • Cross-indemnification + additional-insured endorsements.
  • Participant accident & general liability policies sized to headcount/activities.

4) PUBLIC VS PRIVATE OPS
  • Website: "Private, members-only; by application/invitation; not open to the general public."
  • No public ticket sales pages (no Eventbrite/Airbnb-style listings).
  • Intake: application → approval → membership agreement → dues/payment → RSVP.
  • On-site signage & roster: "Private Event — Members & Invited Guests Only."

5) LAND USE & BUILDING (DO NOT SKIP)
  • Confirm zoning + permitted uses (Ag District) via:
    Zoning & Land Use hub · County Code Ch. 25 · Zone Maps · TMK + Maps hub
  • For any structures: verify if exempt vs. permit-required in EPIC.
    EPIC help library · Building Division home
  • Fire/sanitation/egress: have plans for water, toilets, waste, parking, and noise control (document in your packet).

6) TAXES & FILINGS
  • Register/maintain GET for revenue (PMA +/or LLC as appropriate).
    GET info + register
  • If ANY consideration is for lodging/overnight stays:
    State TAT overview · County 3% TAT info · County TAT payment portal
  • File TA-1/TA-2 (State) and County portion.
  • Keep clean books separating PMA member dues vs. LLC public sales.

7) OPERATIONS PLAYBOOK (MEMBERS)
  • Member folder: signed agreement, waiver, emergency contact, medical/allergy notes.
  • Event SOP: safety brief, boundaries, substance policy, photo/recording rules, quiet hours, wildlife & terrain cautions.
  • Incident log + after-action review for each event (kept private).

8) COMMS & BRAND
  • External channels = education/storytelling about land & legacy; no "public accommodation" invites.
  • All retreat comms route through PMA application/approval workflow.
